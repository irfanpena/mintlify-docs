<Update label="2025-05-17" tags={["2025"]}>
<Tabs>
<Tab title="CVI">
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="New Features" icon="plus-large">
No features were added in this release.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Enhancements" icon="wrench">
* **Phoenix 3**:
    * Increased Animator frame rate from 27fps to 32fps, significantly boosting smoothness.
    * Reduced Animator cold boot time by 60% (from 5s to 2s), and warm boot now consistently under 3 seconds for the Phoenix step.
    * Lipsync accuracy improved by ~22% (AVSR metric)
    * Resolved blurriness and choppiness at conversation start.
    * Enhanced listening mode with more natural micro expressions (eyebrow movements, subtle gestures).
    * Greenscreen mode speed boosted by an additional ~1.5fps.
    * Resolved visual artifacts in 4K videos on Apple devices (codec adjusted to H.265), eliminating black spot artifacts in thumbnails.
* Enhanced CVI Audio Quality: Audio clicks significantly attenuated, providing clearer conversational audio.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
